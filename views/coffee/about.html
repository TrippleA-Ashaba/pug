COFFEE
for EVERYBODY
magalacoffee.com

Since our founding in 2009, our passion has always been bringing you gourmet coffees from all over the world, roasted fresh to order in eco-friendly Loring™ Smart Roasters. We’re committed to quality, service, integrity, approachability, and sustainability. This means more top-notch coffee in your cup, more money in your wallet, and less impact on our environment.

Our coffees are roasted fresh to order, immediately packaged, nitrogen flushed, and shipped directly to your door. We want our customers to experience fresh coffee at peak drinkability, just a few days after being roasted to perfection.

We’ve built our business by roasting exceptional Coffee for Everybody™, whether you’re a coffee connoisseur or just looking for a better cup to start your morning.

We have been fortunate enough to be named one of Inc. 500’s fastest-growing private businesses in the country in 2015 and 2016, and in 2017, we made the top 5000! This, however, is the beginning of our impact. Our dedication to our customers and our partners at origin continue to drive our business and our success.

Give us a try. We think you’ll find that one cup is all it takes to fall in love with Fresh Roasted Coffee.
